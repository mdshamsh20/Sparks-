Motive

Spark is working for the welfare of the student community, and our goal is to spread a healthy and convenient method of stress-free learning. Working in groups and interacting with fellow learners will help the mental health as well as skill level of beginners of today’s generation. Being a student, we want to create a bubble where people can have excess to multiple courses and services, like hand-made notes and room finding facility. Our goal is to expand our network beyond our own college and make Spark a global place for the nourishment of students. It’s our duty as learners and students to help everyone in the field with the problems we are facing ourselves.
